  • Outlet condition
  • Load requirements
  • Replacement materials
  • Diagnostic fee
  • Urgency level

Wire and circuit repair notes

Wire-related repair concerns may involve access, circuit tracing, fixture wiring, older wiring, or safety-sensitive symptoms.
