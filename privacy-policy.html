1. Overview

{{companyName}} is an independent provider-matching platform that helps homeowners organize electrical project details and compare independent provider options. This website does not perform electrical work directly, employ electricians for homeowner projects, supervise providers, or guarantee provider work.

This Privacy Policy describes the types of information that may be collected when you visit the website, submit a request, interact with a form, or contact the platform for support.

2. Information We May Collect

Depending on how you use the website, we may collect information you choose to provide, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • ZIP code or general service location
  • Electrical project category
  • Short project details submitted through a form
  • General support or contact messages

We may also collect basic technical information such as browser type, device type, pages visited, general usage activity, and cookie preference choices.

3. How Information May Be Used

Information submitted through this website may be used to:

  • Organize your electrical provider comparison request
  • Respond to questions or support messages
  • Help match your request with relevant independent provider categories
  • Improve website usability and form experience
  • Remember cookie or privacy preference choices
  • Maintain website security and prevent misuse

Submission of a form does not guarantee provider availability, pricing, response time, or project acceptance.

4. Information Sharing

Information you submit may be used to support independent provider comparison and may be shared with relevant parties only as needed to help process or organize a request. Independent providers are separate businesses and are not employees of {{companyName}}.

Homeowners are responsible for verifying license, insurance, permits, quote details, pricing, timeline, and warranty terms before hiring any provider.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

This website may use cookies or localStorage for basic website functionality, such as remembering cookie preference choices. The cookie banner choice may be stored in your browser using the configured storage key.

You can clear cookies or localStorage through your browser settings. Doing so may cause the cookie banner or preference notices to appear again.

6. Your Choices

You may choose not to submit a contact form. You may also contact the platform to ask general questions about information you previously submitted.

Browser settings may allow you to block cookies, delete stored preferences, or limit certain tracking technologies.

7. Data Security

Reasonable efforts may be used to protect information submitted through the website. However, no website, internet transmission, or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Children’s Privacy

This website is intended for adults seeking electrical provider comparison information. It is not intended for children, and children should not submit personal information through this website.

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
